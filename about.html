Ideals

I specialize in physics based programming, the main focus of all my contributions or work is related to the movement of entities, interactions and collisions between two objects & any outside factors such as collision response, materials that effect traction / friction / drag and monitoring the performance of said tasks. I've had experience with physics systems in both Unity and Unreal as well as across multiple different genres such as top-down 2D shooters, first person 3D sports games and sidescrolling platformers.

One of my best qualities is problem solving, specifically to help bridge the gap between programmers and other areas of the game, such as art, sound and design. Alot of my experience when working as a team has been the middle-man to make sure anything implemented into the game doesn't cause issues and works as it was intended to. While working on personal projects, I try my best to implement non-programming aspects myself before attempting to use placeholders and other external assets, giving me hands on experience in combining creative design and code.
